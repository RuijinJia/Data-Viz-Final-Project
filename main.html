About the Data & Project

Air pollution is the leading public health issue and is one of the main causes of death across the world. Pollution-related diseases include stroke, heart disease, lung cancer, and other respiratory illness. According to World Health Organization’s research, 9 out of 10 people breathe air containing high levels of pollutants. The project is going to look at the details of the aggregate picture of air pollution, explore the causes, the health impact, and future pathways of solutions.
